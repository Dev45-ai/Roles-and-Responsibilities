# Role Name : Director - Performance, Accountability & Service Framework

**The Customer & Outcome Owner: Turning company strategy into retention, expansion, customer value, and revenue outcomes.**

---

📅 Current Date: March 24, 2026
🗂️ Customer Success, Revenue Expansion & Strategic Accounts
🧭 Customer Point of View

---

> **Overarching Goal:** To convert organizational strategy into measurable business outcomes by protecting existing customers, growing account value, improving customer trust, and expanding long-term revenue.

> **Growth Killer to Avoid:** Becoming reactive and account-servicing only. This role fails when it protects today's revenue but does not build tomorrow's customer expansion, strategic account growth, and durable relationships.

---

## 🎯 Success Signal

Customers stay, grow, and trust the company more deeply. Strategy becomes visible in revenue expansion, better retention, and stronger strategic accounts.

## ⚠️ Failure Signal

Revenue depends on constant new hunting because existing accounts do not expand, risks are caught late, and customer value is not being converted into durable growth.

- VP / CEO
- Associate Directors & Delivery Leaders
- Sales / Business Development
- Solution / Presales
