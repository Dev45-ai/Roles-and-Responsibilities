### 💡 Pro Tip: The ENTERPRISE Framework
When architecting enterprise capabilities, always use the **ENTERPRISE** framework:
- **E**nterprise: How does this capability serve the entire organization?
- **N**eeds: What strategic business needs does this capability address?
- **T**ransform: How will this capability transform business outcomes?
- **E**nd-to-End: How does this capability connect across the enterprise?
- **R**esults: What measurable business outcomes will this capability deliver?
- **P**rinciples: What architectural principles guide this capability design?
- **R**igor: How will we ensure quality and consistency in implementation?
- **I**ntegration: How does this capability integrate with other enterprise capabilities?
- **S**trategy: How does this capability align with strategic business goals?
- **E**volution: How will this capability evolve to meet future business needs?
