# Role Name : Head of Department - Performance, Accountability & Service Framework

**The Functional Capability Owner: Building strong teams, strong standards, and reliable delivery performance within a department such as QA, Cloud, Development, UI/UX, or Data.**

---

📅 Current Date: March 24, 2026
🗂️ Department Capability, Standards & Team Growth
🧭 Functional Leadership

---

> **Overarching Goal:** To build a capable, scalable department that delivers high technical quality, develops people, enforces standards, and reliably supports project success.

> **Growth Killer to Avoid:** Being only a senior specialist or only a people manager. This role fails when it neglects either delivery performance or long-term department capability building.

---

## 🎯 Success Signal

The department is respected for strong quality, strong people, and dependable support to the business — not just for individual experts.

## ⚠️ Failure Signal

The department depends on a few heroes, standards vary by person, and project teams suffer from slow staffing, weak quality control, or skill gaps.

## 👥 Main Interfaces

- Associate Director / Director
- Project & Delivery Leaders
- Presales / Solution Teams
- HR / Training / Operations
- Department Team Members
