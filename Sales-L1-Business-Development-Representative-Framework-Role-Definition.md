## Core BDR Responsibilities

**Operational Focus:** Your execution quality directly determines the pipeline health and efficiency of the sales process.

Prospecting & Lead Identification

| Activity | Execution Standard | Key Performance Indicators |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Prospect Identification** | Identify potential leads through LinkedIn, email, phone calls, and partner channels using ICP criteria | 50+ new leads per week with proper firmographic/demographic fit |
| **Initial Outreach** | Use pre-approved scripts and decks; personalize for each prospect; multi-channel approach (email, phone, LinkedIn) | 20%+ response rate; ≤48h follow-up cadence |
| **Lead Capture** | Capture all leads in CRM immediately with complete contact information and source details | 100% lead capture rate; ≤2h from initial contact |

Lead Qualification & Handoff

| Activity | Execution Standard | Key Performance Indicators |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Initial Qualification Calls** | Conduct 15-20 minute discovery calls; use BANT framework; identify pain points and needs | 35%+ conversion to qualified leads; 10+ qualification calls daily |
| **CRM Data Management** | Enter/update all lead information; maintain communication logs; update lead stage; document client needs | 100% CRM accuracy; ≤2h data entry; weekly status updates |
| **Transfer to AE** | Provide complete CRM details; share company materials; coordinate demo handoffs; ensure proper data transfer to AE/CSM | 95%+ complete handoffs; ≤24h transfer time; 80%+ AE acceptance rate |

## Productivity Killers & Fixes

**Based on 2024 BDR Performance Research** – These are proven time-wasters that sabotage pipeline generation.

| Killer | Impact | Simple Fix |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Manual Data Entry** | BDRs spend 30%+ time on CRM updates (Gartner 2024) | **→ "CRM First" Rule:** - Log interactions IMMEDIATELY after call/email - Use voice-to-text for faster notes - Leverage CRM templates for common entries |
| **Unfocused Prospecting** | 45% of outreach goes to unqualified targets (Sales Hacker) | **→ "ICP Validation" System:** - Start each day with 10 min ICP review - Use firmographic filters before outreach - Track conversion rates by segment weekly |
| **Poor Handoff Process** | 35% of SQLs stall due to incomplete information (Forrester) | **→ "Handoff Checklist" Protocol:** - Mandatory fields before transfer: pain points, budget, timeline - 5-min sync with AE before handoff - Track "Handoff Score" in team dashboard |

## Team Amplification Tactics

**How BDRs Elevate the Entire Sales Organization** – Move from individual contributor to pipeline multiplier.

**Impact:** Reduces handoff delays by 50% and ensures alignment on priority accounts

CRM Health Dashboard

Real-time view of lead status, conversion rates, and data completeness across the team

**Impact:** Identifies bottlenecks immediately; drives accountability for data accuracy

Learn & Assist Program

Shadow AEs on discovery calls; assist CSMs with expansion opportunities; document learnings

**Impact:** Accelerates career growth while improving lead quality through real-world insights

## Critical "Don'ts" for BDRs

- **🚫 Never** skip CRM entry to "save time"  
  *(Creates pipeline blind spots and forecast inaccuracies)*
- **🚫 Never** hand off leads without complete qualification information  
  *(Wastes AE time and damages credibility)*
- **🚫 Never** use unapproved messaging or deviate from ICP criteria  
  *(Creates misaligned opportunities that stall in pipeline)*
